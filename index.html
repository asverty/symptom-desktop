NOT
CONCEPT

The concept is that there is no concept and this gives me complete freedom. I don’t attach myself to anything, I have a lot of space for thought,because I’m still at the beginning of the way: I study transforming and this is reflected in the design. I experiment a lot with forms, combine different textures and minerals. This technique can be seen in rings with large stones. They can not be repeated, because it uses natural stone, a copy of which can never be found. My jewelry is not distributed by gender - each time it warms my soul to see a guy with a cuff or ring of my design.
